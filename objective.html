Services: Prime Minister's Youth Skill Development Program

In line with directions of current Government, NAVTTC is Primarily Focused to prepare skilled manpower for national mega projects like CPEC and other energy related project as CPEC is expected to generate aprox, 2 million jobs for skilled manpower, since 2013 NAVTTC has equipped approximately 100,000 jobless individual with hands-on skills and this year another 100,000 youth will be empowered through skill development to uplift their socio-economic conditions of the country and contribute towards economic development of country.
